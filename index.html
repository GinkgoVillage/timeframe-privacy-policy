Time Frame – Changes Over Time: Privacy Policy

Welcome to Time Frame – Changes Over Time. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and secure your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Identification Data

We do not collect any personal identification information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

1.2 On-Device Processing and Storage

Your photos and generated images are stored locally on your device only.

We do not upload or transmit images to any server.

2. Camera and Photo Library Permissions

Camera Access

To take photos for time-based tracking, the app requests access to your device camera.
We do not collect or store your camera content externally.

Photo Library Access

To save generated images, the app requests access to your photo library.
Your media is not shared, uploaded, or otherwise collected.

Permission is requested strictly for these functional purposes. You may disable permissions in device settings, which may limit functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We only use information stored locally for functions such as:

  • Generating time-lapse images
  • Creating and displaying your timeline
  • Saving and browsing past images

We do not use data for ads, analytics, or third-party sharing.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect content stored on your device, but:

  • We do not upload data to any external servers
  • We cannot prevent data loss if your device is lost or damaged
  • We recommend that you back up important images locally or to your own cloud

5. Third-Party Services

The app may utilize built-in system components (e.g., iOS camera and photo frameworks) to enable features, but these do not result in collection of personal data.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed at children. If you are a parent or guardian and your child uses this app, please supervise usage and delete any local data as needed via app settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to app features. Updated policies will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
